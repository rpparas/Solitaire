  • Overview [top]
    Solitaire is one of the oldest card games in the world. The traditional solitaire is played by a single player, whose aim is to complete the line-up of the four suits in ascending order from ace to king. The game play involves seven piles of cards – each initially stacked with cards in increasing order from 1 to 7, left to right. Only the topmost card in the pile is visible.

  • Objective [top]
    A player is allowed to draw three (3) cards from his deck (Stock) which are then placed in the Drawn Cards area.  A player is also allowed to move cards across card holders or components in accordance with the Rules. At the end of the day, the player's aim is to send all the cards from the Pile and Stock to the Cabinet (Waste Pile).

  • Rules [top]
    Any transfer of card to the Cabinet (from Pile or Drawn Cards) can only be done one at a time.  Similarly, any transfer from the Drawn Cards is taken one at a time.  On the other hand, moves within the Piles area can involve a single or multiple transfer of cards, provided that they are faced up.

    The basic rule is that the source card (if multiple, the topmost one), should have a rank which is one-lower than the destination card or Pile, and secondly, the source and destination must be of different colors.

    Example:  7 Hearts can be transferred to a Pile with lowest card of 8 Spade or 8 Club. Queen of Clubs can be received by King of Hearts or Diamonds.

    Consequently, only Kings can be transferred to a blank or empty Pile.

  • Strategy [top]
    The main strategy of the game is to build a pile of cards of alternating colors and decreasing rank.  This is done by transferring all cards from the Stock and flipping the face down cards in the Piles.

  • Winning [top]
    How do you actually win?  You have to clear the Piles of cards and empty the Stock to win the game.  But to qualify for the Best Times, the game duration should fall amongst the five fastest players in record.  To bid for Fast Moves, you should aim for the smallest number of moves in this game.
