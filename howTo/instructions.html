Java Solitaire

Help...

  • Starting [top]
    The game automatically loads a randomly drawn arrangement of cards.  The game starts at the instant you press on any of the card components.  To restart, navigate to the menu bar and select Game, then Deal.

  • First Moves [top]
    If a low-rank card happens to be flipped face up in one of the Piles, you may send this card directly to the Cabinet using double-click.  Then after, you may choose to move the card/s according to the rules of the game.

    Most transfers would usually move the faced up cards, hence, you need to flip the faced down cards to reveal their contents.

  • Draw Cards [top]
    The next step is to utilize the cards contained in the Stock.  Clicking on the Stock's button, the program draws three cards and places them in the Drawn Cards area.  The player can select the topmost card and the player may send this to one of the Cabinets or the Piles.  The cards underneath the top cannot be used for game play.

  • Moving from Drawn Cards [top]
    If you wish to move a card from the Drawn Cards area to the Cabinet, just double click it.  On the other hand, if you intend to move it to a Pile, then drag it a few pixels from its position.  If only one pile can receive that card, then it is moved automatically to that pile.  Otherwise, an option dialog is presented before you.

  • Moving Within Piles [top]
    Any transfer to the Cabinet requires a double click.  Transferring cards amongst piles, requires that you select the the source card and drag it to the destination pile's last card.

  • Releasing from Cabinet (Waste Pile) [top]
    As part of a strategy, you may move a card from the Cabinet and send it (back) to the Pile.  You may do so by clicking the corresponding button which contains the card.  An option dialog is brought up when necessary.
